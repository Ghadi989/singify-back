# Singify — User Guide

## What is Singify?

Singify turns any song into a karaoke version. Search for a track, wait about 90 seconds while the vocals are removed by AI, then sing along with synchronized lyrics.

---

## Finding a Song

Type any artist or song title in the search bar at the top of the page. Results come from Spotify's catalogue — millions of tracks available.

---

## Playing a Song

Click any song to start. What happens next depends on whether the karaoke version is ready:

| State | What you hear | Right panel |
|---|---|---|
| **First time** | Spotify 30-second preview (loops) | "Getting your karaoke version…" spinner |
| **Karaoke ready** | Full instrumental (vocals removed) | Synchronized lyrics |
| **Already processed** | Instrumental plays immediately | Synchronized lyrics |

Processing takes roughly **1–2 minutes**. You can let the preview loop and it will switch automatically when ready.

---

## Recent Songs

The **Recent** tab on the left shows every song you've ever played on Singify. The list is saved in your browser and synced from the server — it survives page refreshes and comes back on any device connected to the same account.

---

## Liked Songs

Click the ♥ icon on any song in the player bar to add it to your **Liked** tab. Your likes are saved to your account.

---

## Tips

- **Song already in the list?** Clicking it again will play the karaoke version instantly if it was processed before — no wait.
- **Lyrics not showing?** Not every track has lyrics in the database. The player still works without them.
- **Preview sounds cut off?** That is the Spotify 30-second clip. The full song plays once the karaoke version is ready.
